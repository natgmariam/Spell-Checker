Syphon Filter is a series of third person shooter video 
games developed by SCE Bend Studio formerly Eidetic 
It follows the missions of covert agent Gabriel Logan and 
his efforts to stop an international conspiracy behind a 
bioterrorism incident The series first launched in nineteen
ninety eight with the PlayStation title Syphon Filter and there have 
since been eight games released across all major platforms 
as well as a few mobile handheld spinoffs The games place 
an emphasis on stealth hand to hand combat and solving 
puzzles over pure action
