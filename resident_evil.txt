Resident Evil is an action adventure horror series from
Capcom It follows the story of a group of people as they
fight off a variety of supernatural creatures and the evil
corporation Umbrella Corporation Players experience the
horror and action through a third person perspective and
must use a variety of weapons and items to survive The
series has seen a multitude of games released and has
remained popular over the years It is intense atmosphere
storyline and unique gameplay make it a stand out title
among horror fans
