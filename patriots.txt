The New England Patriots are a professional American football
team based in Foxborough Massachusetts The team competes in
the National Football League NFL as a member of the American
Football Conference AFC East division Founded in nineteen
fifty nine the Patriots have become one of the most
successful and high profile teams in NFL history The
Patriots have won six Super Bowl titles and seventeen AFC
East titles and are one of just eight teams in NFL history
to appear in at least nine Super Bowls The team is also
known for its strong coaching staff having hired four
different head coaches since its inception Led by quarterback
Tom Brady and head coach Bill Belichick the Patriots have
established themselves as one of the top teams in NFL
history
